about

It's finally spring and I have taken every opportunity to bike around the city. The gratitude of a late spring is so immense— The trees reborn are still pink and yellow and white and purple for what seems to be the longest I've ever experienced.

I started working at Haley Sharpe as what they call an Experiential/Environmental Graphic Designer. There are such wonderful projects to look forward to like re-designing the way finding for a bird trail at Cornell's Ornithology lab. I've been an avid user of the Merlin bird app so this project in particular holds a very special place in my heart. More than anything, I am keen to how this experience is going to shape/change my practice as a maker. It is, after all, a 9-5 that I have to completely commit myself to...
